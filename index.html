About

Serving People with Vision & Commitment

Dedicated to public service, transparent governance, community welfare, and sustainable development for the people of Solapur.

Building a Better Tomorrow Through Public Service

Prathamesh Mahesh Kothe is a dedicated public leader and social worker committed to serving the people of Solapur, Maharashtra. With a vision for inclusive growth, transparent governance, and youth empowerment, he actively works to improve civic infrastructure, strengthen community welfare, and create opportunities for future generations.

As an elected Corporator of Solapur Municipal Corporation (Ward No. 10), he has consistently focused on addressing local issues, improving public services, and ensuring that every citizen's voice is heard. His leadership is guided by integrity, accountability, and people-first governance.

Beyond public office, he actively contributes to social welfare through Vishnu-Mahesh Pratishthan and supports industrial growth, digital infrastructure, and sustainable development through various leadership roles, helping build a stronger and more progressive Solapur.
